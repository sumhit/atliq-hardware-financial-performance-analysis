# 🌍 AtliQ Hardware: Financial & Market Performance Analysis

## 📌 Overview
This project analyzes the global financial performance of AtliQ Hardware using Excel dashboards and P&L reports.

The focus is on understanding:
- Revenue growth across countries
- Cost structure (COGS)
- Profitability (Gross Margin)
- Market performance vs targets

---

## 📊 Key Insights

### 🚀 Growth vs Profitability Trade-off
- Total revenue increased from **$87.5M (2019) to $598.9M (2021)**
- However, Gross Margin % declined from **41.4% to 36.4%**
👉 Indicates aggressive expansion with rising costs

---

### 🌍 Top Performing Markets
- India: **$161.3M revenue** (highest growth market)
- USA: **$87.8M revenue**
- China & UK also showed strong expansion

---

### ⚠️ Target Achievement Analysis
- Global target shortfall: **-$54.9M (-9.2%)**
- Almost all countries underperformed vs targets

#### Worst performing markets:
- Poland: **-18.1%**
- Canada: **-14.5%**
- Spain: **-14.1%**
- Germany: **-12.7%**

#### Relatively better:
- India: **-5.9%** (closest to target)

---

### 📉 Key Business Problems
1. High revenue growth but declining margins  
2. Poor target forecasting (consistent underachievement)  
3. Increasing COGS impacting profitability  
4. Over-expansion in low-performing markets  

---

## 💡 Strategic Recommendations

### 1. Cost Optimization
- Focus on reducing COGS in markets like Germany & Canada

### 2. Market Prioritization
- Invest more in India, USA, China
- Re-evaluate low ROI markets like Poland, Spain

### 3. Pricing Strategy
- Improve pricing in markets with declining GM%

### 4. Better Forecasting
- Align targets with realistic growth trends
